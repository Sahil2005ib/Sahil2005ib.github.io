05.projects

Edge AI Anomaly Detection — Dissertation

2025 — 2026
Split VQ-VAE · Aerospace industry partner (NDA)

Designed and trained a Vector-Quantised Variational Autoencoder with a ResNet18 encoder and EMA-updated codebook for unsupervised anomaly detection across three datasets: industry turbine blades, MVTec AD metal nut, and a custom dataset.

Built a split edge-cloud architecture — encoder runs in an iPhone PWA, decoder on a Flask server. Only ~100 bytes of codebook indices are transmitted per inference, achieving very high compression ratios versus raw image transfer.

Developed the full stack end-to-end: dataset acquisition and labelling, PyTorch training pipeline, REST API in Flask, and the PWA front-end. Benchmarked against a single-device baseline; methodology grounded in current VQ-VAE and split-computing literature (van den Oord, Razavi, Matsubara, BottleFit, FrankenSplit).
